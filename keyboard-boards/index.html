The Keyboard Board

Curated performances. Minimal aesthetic. Maximum class.

The Keyboard Board was born out of necessity; a refined solution to a very practical problem. Designed for musicians and performers working in tight spaces, it transforms the keyboard cover into a functional surface without sacrificing elegance. Instead of cluttering a room with extra furniture, the Keyboard Board creates usable space directly on the instrument itself, balancing form and function with a restrained, classical aesthetic. It is a piece meant to disappear when not noticed and to feel intentional when it is.
